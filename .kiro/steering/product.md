# Product Overview

This is a **Tirupati Trip Management Application** - a web-based platform for organizing and managing group trips to Tirupati, a sacred pilgrimage destination in India.

## Core Features

- **Trip Planning**: Detailed trip information, itinerary, and logistics
- **Member Management**: User authentication, member profiles, and access control
- **Photo Gallery**: Image upload, sharing, and gallery management with lightbox viewing
- **Interactive Map**: Trip route visualization and location mapping
- **Timeline**: Trip events and milestone tracking
- **Authentication**: Secure login system with password recovery

## Target Users

- Trip organizers and coordinators
- Trip participants and members
- Anyone planning or participating in Tirupati pilgrimage trips

## Key User Flows

1. **Member Registration/Login**: Users authenticate to access trip content
2. **Trip Information**: Browse trip details, itinerary, and logistics
3. **Photo Sharing**: Upload and view trip photos in organized galleries
4. **Member Directory**: View other trip participants and their profiles
5. **Map Navigation**: Explore trip routes and important locations

The application emphasizes a warm, spiritual aesthetic with saffron/gold theming appropriate for the religious pilgrimage context.
